Spirit
Chaos - randomness, probability, unstable magic.
Order - structure, law, patterns, and control.
Fog - concealment, moisture, illusions, and disorientation.
Ice - cold, frost, stillness, and preservation.
Stone - distinct from Earth; focuses on minerals, crystals, and density.
Sand - erosion, dunes, abrasion, and shifting terrain.
Metal - forged alloys, magnetism, and conductive power.
Time - aging, acceleration, stasis, rewinding.
Sound - vibration, resonance, shattering waves.
Blood - vitality, sacrifice, lineage, and primal instinct.
Mutation - change, adaptation, unstable evolution.
Bone - structure, endurance, skeletal magic.
Dream - imagination, illusion, subconscious power.
Volcanic - earth, fire, and heat.

Evolved Classes 🌟: 
Martial -
Variant-bound - warrior whose skin slowly turns to their element/variant as they level up.
Siege Savant - battlefield genius who weaponizes improvised structures and terrain.
Creature‑Speaker - bonded to a juvenile creature; grows stronger as the creature matures.
Oath-breaker - gains strength by violating sacred vows in ritualized ways.

Stealth - 
Storm-runner - sprints so fast they generate Elemental storms and shockwaves.
Chronicler of Blades - records battles in enchanted tomes that replay as illusions or ghosts.
Chain Dancer - acrobatic fighter using weighted chains in rhythmic, hypnotic patterns.
Corpse Stalker - cosmic hunter who uses parasitic symbiotes for power.

Crux -
Relic Host - carries a cursed relic that grants power but demands constant appeasement.
Starcaller - channels constellations as living avatars.
Spellforger - crafts unstable combination spells on the fly, each cast reshaping reality unpredictably.
Hollowborn - body inhabited by a sealed spirit; swaps control in combat.

Divine -
Dreamwalker - enters dreams, steals memories, plants ideas, battles nightmares.
Gravemind - communes with the collective memory of the dead, not individual spirits.
Fatemender - rewrites tiny threads of destiny at a cost to their own lifespan.
Bone Shepherd - raises skeletal beasts instead of humanoid undead.

Support -
Bellwarden - uses enchanted bells to control/heal/hinder crowds, repel spirits, or shatter morale.
Echo Antagonizer - fights alongside time‑shifted versions of themselves.
Inkweaver - tattoos come alive as summoned creatures or weapons.
Mask Maker - crafts magical masks that can mirror other classes.
