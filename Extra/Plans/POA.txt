One‑sentence purpose:
Daily tasks turned into quests and adventures.

Core theme:
This is a daily planner/event manager. What you do throughout the day will give you a bonus to your rewards.
At the end of the day it will generate a story you can read or participate in like a choose your own quest. A cozy way to end or start the day.

Story setting 🌟
--------------------------------
Our story is set in a slightly distant future about 200 years from now. Multiple Species across the Milky Way Galaxy have acquired the capability to travel between star systems in moments by folding space. Whichever way each group gets to the concept, the idea is that the universe itself is held together by an underlying force. Humans call it "The Strong Nuclear Force". The energy that holds all things together became a source of power that unravels the mysteries of the universe and grant power beyond our concept of physics today. However the group gets to it, the power is generated from a strong‑force coupling and is used as a controlled, non‑destructive energy to tap generally referred to as 'The Prevailing Crux'.  It allows a leap in technology and advancement to each species that can commune with it. Now as different species expand and explore more and more derelict planets of old cities are being found strong in crux energy with their own mysteries to unfold.
