Stats 🌟
Species :
Health - Total hit points before the character is knocked out. Example: 1 point = 20 health
Resource - A pool used for abilities, spells, or strenuous actions. Example: 1 point = 20 energy/mana/rage/stamina
Agility - Governs movement speed, dodge chance, and finesse. Example: 1 point = 2% dodge + speed
Strength - Determines physical power and melee weapon damage. Example: 1 point = 5% physical damage
Stealth - Ability to avoid detection, ambush enemies, or move unseen. Example: 1 point = 5% stealth damage   
Intelligence - Controls magical potency, skill ceiling, and complex problem‑solving. Example: 1 point = 5% magic damage
Charisma - Influences conversation outcomes, persuasion, and charm effects. Example: 1 point = 5% charm + speech skill
Resilience - Defense against damage and resistance to harmful effects. Example: 1 point = 3% defense

Skill tree 🌟 
Points will be earned through activity or time spent in game.
Three parallel trees grow at the same time: Task, Profession, and Class.
Each tree is tiered (Early → Mid → Advanced → Epic → Legendary) with branching choices.
Nodes come in types like passive boosts, active abilities, utilities, transformations, and specializations.
Growth sources differ: Tasks grow from daily habits, Profession from your trade, Class from combat and exploration.
Buildings and teachers unlock higher tiers.
Monster kills and expeditions feed the Class tree with essences, abilities, and combat perks.
Profession mastery unlocks recipes, efficiencies, tools, and combat‑adjacent perks tied to your trade.
Cross‑tree empowerment lets one tree enhance another, creating hybrid builds and personalized growth paths.

Profession Map 🌟
Each upgrade increases task rewards of that profession by 2% per tier and gives a stat boost of + 1  to specified stat. Higher tiers unlock new patters or the need to seek a teacher to move up.
Novice - Entry-level familiarity; basic tools and methods. XP Required: 0
Trained - Understands fundamentals and completes standard tasks reliably. XP Required: 300
Adept - Handles difficult or time-consuming tasks with confidence. XP Required: 750
Expert - High-level mastery; innovates and produces exceptional work. XP Required: 2000
Instructor - Able to teach, mentor, and formalize knowledge. XP Required: 5000
Virtuoso - Signature mastery; world-class, recognizable excellence. XP Required: 10000
