Gear can be obtained in battle or created at a city building. Some weapon buffs must be unlocked by disenchanting gear. Non-armor gear is infused with an enchantment of choice and can be made with or improved with higher quality materials.  

The build goes: 
Material + Object + Buff = Nanosteel chest plate with pockets

Buffs -
Stat & Attribute Buffs :
Jellyfish - extra health
Panda - extra energy/mana/rage/stamina
Falcon - extra agility
Elephant - extra strength
Chameleon - extra stealth
Octopus - extra intelligence
Fox - extra charisma
Turtle - extra resilience
Badger - increases courage / fear resistance
Hawk‑Eye - boosts accuracy / crit chance
Bull - increases raw damage
Moth - increases evasion / dodge chance
Porcupine - returns physical damage

Utility & Movement Buffs :
Pockets - snacks in your pockets give you energy
Bubble - every point extends beyond your body (aura radius, shield radius, etc.)
Float - floating points give hover speed
Silent - reduces footstep noise
Swift - faster item use / reload / interaction speed 
Cloud - reduces fall damage
Blink_thread - short-range teleport cooldown reduction
Trailblazer - increases movement speed after traveling in a straight line
Frost_walking - leaves slowing nanites behind you

Combat & Damage Buffs :
Leech - increases life‑steal
Focus - increases spell power or precision
Spider - boosts poison potency
Snake - poison damage
Stormcharge - adds lightning damage to attacks
Embercore - adds fire damage to attacks
Frostbite - adds cold damage to attacks
Earthrend - adds earth damage to attacks
Empty_shatter - adds void damage to attacks
Tiger - increases bleed chance
Penguin - increases damage to stunned or frozen enemies
Cheetah - boosts attack speed for a short time after ability use
Infused - increases Crux generation or potency

Defensive & Survival Buffs :
Reinforced - increases armor / reduces incoming physical damage
Iron_root - increases stability / stagger resistance
Guarded - reduces ranged damage taken
Ward - increases shield strength
Starfish - passive health regeneration
Flare - small damage burst when shield breaks
Crux_proofing - reduces magic damage
Push - periodic shockwave that pushes enemies back
Heart_warming - increases healing received
Nanite_Weave - boosts nanite regeneration or efficiency

Special & Thematic Buffs :
Cat - increases loot quality
Gremlin - increases crafting speed or salvage yield
Mantis - increases crit chance for abilities
Specter - increases invisibility duration
Wisp - increases light radius or detection radius 
Intwined - increases revive speed or reduces death penalty
Chrono_Slip - reduces cooldowns slightly
Magpie - increases currency gain
Voidtouched - small chance to negate any incoming hit
Phoenix - revive with a small amount of health once per battle
