Castle Proper - The heart of your domain: throne room, main hall, and administrative center. Improves renown gain and increases follower capacity. Core actions include holding court, assigning followers, and reviewing reports.
Stables - Home for mounts and couriers. Boosts travel speed and delivery efficiency. Lets you feed, train, and assign mounts for tasks or errands.
Market - A bustling trade hub for buying, selling, and commissioning goods. Provides better trade prices and access to merchants and traders.
Smith - The forge and repair center for gear. Enhances crafting quality and reduces repair costs. Supports forging, repairing, and upgrading equipment.
School / Library - A center of knowledge and research. Increases research speed and skill XP. Allows studying, researching, and teaching followers.

Outer Grounds - 
Farms - Crop production, cooking boosts, gathering bonuses.
Dairy - Milk and cheese production, animal care improvements.
Masons - Construction, repairs, and structural upgrades.
Hunt / Fish Outpost - Hunting, fishing, stealth, and food gathering.
Traveling Market - Rare goods, special quests, rotating merchants.
Event Grounds - Festivals, seasonal events, morale boosts, social XP.

Out There - 
Wild Lands - Dangerous wilderness full of rare resources, threats, and exploration XP.
Friend Home - Visit a friend’s castle for diplomacy, trading, or cooperative quests.
Expeditions - Long-term missions where followers earn XP, loot, and renown.
Other Countries - Distant lands with exotic goods, unique quests, and diplomacy.
New Flora / Fauna - Discovery zones for research, rare plants, magical creatures.
Road Building - Infrastructure expansion that improves travel and unlocks new regions.
