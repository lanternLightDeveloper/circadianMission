Deep History 🌟
Their home can be called a land of large things. Compared to earth the flora and fauna never got the memo to get small. Evolving from a larger species the Owliens lost some of their size as their brains evolved. Their cities are vast expanses in a variety of elements from mountains that house millions of people to forest cities. A species that focused exclusively on technology they are much younger than humans, but just as ambitious to expand through the stars.
