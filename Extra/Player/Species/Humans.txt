Deep History 🌟
The human race is a species of slow progress. They have dealt with collapse over and again. A primate based species we expanded across a vast variety of terrains and climates. Persaverance is the premier word for our species. We fought ourselves more than anything to grow and achieve our goals. Taking hundreds of thousands of years to reach for the stars, in our historical 22nd century changed everything.

Their absolute need to figure out "what is this" led them to the "Empty Space Theory". The step was actually an explosive expansion to the next generation of technology. We got lost for a hundred years cleaning the mess of our previous generations before we were reconvinced to look up. We have begun to use the new physics to create ships that fold space to explore places our satellites could map and our old travel would have taken hundreds of years, now it only takes a few seconds. So we are are out here looking for questions that we never thought to ask.  Our ideas may vary, but our questions will never end.
