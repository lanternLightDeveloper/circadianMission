Deep History 🌟
The human race is a species of slow progress. They have dealt with collapse over and again. Their absolute need to figure out "what is this" led them to the "Empty Space Theory". From fire to the theory of empty space humans could not stop looking at everything around them.. We are humans all of our differences make us. Our ideas may vary, but our questions will never end.
