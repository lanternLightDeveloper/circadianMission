Deep History 🌟
The human race is a species of slow progress. We have dealt with collapse over and again. Fueled by self and the environment, we struggled for every day. A primate based species we expanded across a broad range of variety of terrains and climates. Perseverance is the premier word for our species. We fought ourselves more than anything to grow and achieve our goals. Taking hundreds of thousands of years to reach for the stars. In our historical 22nd century a breakthrough in physics changed everything.

Their absolute need to figure out "what is this" led them to the "Empty Space Theory". This allowed humans to interact with the universe in way we dreamed of in fantasy. The technology is so advanced that it seems like magic the way they control fire, plant, water air or any of the other permutations the Prevailing Crux Theory allows. War and disease ended. The step was an explosive expansion to the next generation of technology. We got lost for a hundred years cleaning the messes of our previous generations before we were reconvinced to look up. We have begun to use the new physics to create ships that fold space. We have been gifted with the ability to explore places our satellites could map. A place our old forms of travel would have taken hundreds of years, now it only takes a few seconds. So we are are out here looking for questions that we never thought to ask.  Our ideas may vary, but our questions will never end.
