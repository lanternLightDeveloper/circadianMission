Deep History 🌟
A mycelia based species that evolved on a planet of mostly forests. Their spores allow them to communicate deep ideas and messages. over time they cultivated different spore structures that could serve many purposes. One of the spore types was of knowledge. It allowed them to pass information from one generation to the next. When their population hit a saturation point even the children are born knowing the history of the earliest generations. Spores even became their form of nanites emitting them from their bodies to be almost like living elementals. Being a super organic race they carve their ships out of asteroids in an attempt to reach out to the stars in their unified spore theory.
