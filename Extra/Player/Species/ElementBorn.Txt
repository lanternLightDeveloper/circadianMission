Deep History 🌟
Born in a system of three suns their home world is a planet of storms and unpredictability. They evolved from primates and struggled on the surface until they found vast underground caves. They moved underground to survive. The storms made them vastly adaptive. They focused all of their energy to creating technology to help them back to the surface. As they advanced they  grew to a point where moving back to the surface was within reach. What was on the surface was more chaotic than their ancestors wrote of. They pushed themselves to adapt further then generations of adaptive magic blended their species with the storms that constantly bombard their planet. 

Defensive technology blended with a need to meld to the situation gives them different capabilities depending on the storm they are born in. Nobody is sure if one of their scientist found The Prevailing Crux theory or if being melded with the storms wired the knowledge into them. The moment they could they created a giant fleet of colony ships to take them to a new home. While they left their home the founded life in a new signle star system to to give them a chance to grow their population more than a few billion. Far from the chaos of their home, their past had made them wWorshippers of chaos. The need for each other to survive made them great companions, even if they try to convince you to destroy everything all of the time.
