Deep History 🌟
Born in a system of three suns their home world is a planet of storms. They evolved from primates and moved underground to survive. As their adaptation magic advanced they moved back to the surface. Generations of adaptive magic blended their species with the storms that constantly bombard their planet. This gives them different capabilities depending on the storm they are born in.. Worshippers of chaos who make great companions, even if they try to convince you to destroy everything all of the time.
