Deep History 🌟
The Oozonites are a species of living ooze. They are a hive mind and are very resource hungry. They are a threat to all life, but not intentionally. They are hungry like without the sense of aggression. they just think that all things need to be absorbed into The Great Ooze. They come from an ocean planet with little land. There were a handful of different tribe minds until the Oozonite consumed the others becoming the dominant life form. Every Oozonite you see is all part of one life form. It sends bits of itself out to experience the universe. Thousands of years of fighting to the top made this a war loving race who is stuck in constant consumption. While they seem like they are ready to fight always, its because they do. Fighting to this species is how care is show. Not to hurt or kill, they love through attack.
