Social, Utility & Support Roles 
-------------------------------------------
Seer - Diviner - Oracle
-------------------------------------------
Seer : visionary who perceives the hidden flows of Crux, glimpses possible futures, and manipulates fate through ritual and intuition. Seers blend prophetic insight with nanite‑enhanced perception, granting them unparalleled battlefield awareness and reality‑bending abilities.

Diviner : master of fate, probability, and cosmic patterns who manipulates outcomes, shields allies from doom, and rewrites destiny.

Oracle : transcendent prophet who channels pure Crux visions, unleashes devastating revelations, and commands battlefield‑wide insight.

-------------------------------------------
Enchanter - Controller - Bard
-------------------------------------------
Enchanter :  master of Crux‑woven magic, emotional resonance, and nanite‑augmented influence. Enchanters manipulate minds, moods, and momentum, empowering allies while subtly undermining foes. Their spells shape the flow of battle without relying on raw damage.

Controller :  battlefield manipulator who uses illusions, telekinetic Crux, and nanite‑based crowd control to dominate enemy movement and actions.

Bard :  charismatic performer who channels Crux through music, rhythm, and storytelling, empowering allies and weakening foes with emotional resonance.

-------------------------------------------
Engineer - Artificer - Mortarist 
-------------------------------------------
Engineer : brilliant technomancer who blends mechanical ingenuity with Crux‑powered devices and programmable nanites. Engineers deploy gadgets, turrets, drones, and battlefield tools that reshape combat through precision, automation, and controlled chaos.

Artificer : master builder who constructs advanced turrets, drones, and hardlight machinery to dominate the battlefield with precision automation.

Mortarist : long‑range demolition expert who uses explosive shells, arc‑launchers, and nanite‑fused munitions to reshape the battlefield from afar. 

-------------------------------------------
