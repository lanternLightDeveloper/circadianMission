Crux & Elemental Magic 🌟
Sorcerer - Chronomancer - Nano-caster 
Sorcerer - conduit of raw Crux energy who channels the universe’s binding force into destructive, protective, or reality‑altering spells. Sorcerers wield unstable power amplified by nanite regulators, balancing overwhelming potential with inherent risk.
Chronomancer - manipulator of time who bends causality, accelerates or slows reality, and weaponizes temporal distortion.
Nano-caster  - technomancer who commands swarms of programmable nanites, shaping them into weapons, constructs, and devastating clouds.

Warlock - Necromancer - Monster Summoner
Warlock - wielder of forbidden Crux channels and unstable nanite rituals. Warlocks tap into the darker, more volatile aspects of the universe’s binding force, bending entropy, decay, and living constructs to their will. Their power is immense - but always comes with risk.
Necromancer - master of death, decay, and Crux‑animated corpses. Manipulates entropy to raise the dead and drain the living.
Monster Summoner - bio‑engineer of horrors who uses nanites to construct, mutate, and command monstrous allies.

Druid - Shaman - Beast Summoner 
Druid - bio‑Crux channeler who manipulates living energy, nanite‑grown flora, and primal cosmic forces. Druids blend ancient instinct with futuristic bio‑engineering, shaping the battlefield with regenerative fields, living constructs, and elemental Crux surges.
Shaman - spiritual conduit who channels ancestral Crux, elemental forces, and spirit‑echoes to heal, empower, and devastate.
Beast Summoner - primal bio‑engineer who grows, mutates, and commands living nanite‑beasts infused with Crux energy.

Evolved Crux 🌟
Relic Host - carries a cursed relic that grants power but demands constant appeasement.
Starcaller - channels constellations as living avatars.
Spellforger - crafts unstable combination spells on the fly, each cast reshaping reality unpredictably.
Hollowborn - body inhabited by a sealed spirit; swaps control in combat.
