Team - This can be a set number of people to have with you for quests.
Companion is a person or friend or partner. One of your close people.
Followers are people in your village who are devout to you. 
Profession mate is a person from your work place that you have bonded with. They may invite you to events or you can invite 
them to grow them into a follower/companion.
Townspeople can be paid or asked to do tasks or go on expeditions or change their job.
Zombies are people/zombies collected from battle. People would be unusable until they fail or pass a set amount of days with a 
percent chance to join or be removed.


Stats-
Health - Total hit points before the character is knocked out. Example: 85
Stamina/Mana - Resource pool used for abilities, spells, or strenuous actions. Example: 40
Agility - Governs movement speed, dodge chance, and finesse. Example: 12
Strength - Determines physical power and melee weapon damage. Example: 15
Intelligence - Controls magical potency, skill ceiling, and complex problem‑solving. Example: 18
Charisma - Influences conversation outcomes, persuasion, and charm effects. Example: 10
Resilience - Defense against damage and resistance to harmful effects. Example: 14
Stealth - Ability to avoid detection, ambush enemies, or move unseen. Example: 9


Skill tree- points will be earned through activity or time spent in game.
Three parallel trees grow at the same time: Task, Profession, and Class.
Each tree is tiered (Early → Mid → Advanced → Epic → Legendary) with branching choices.
Nodes come in types like passive boosts, active abilities, utilities, transformations, and specializations.
Growth sources differ: Tasks grow from daily habits, Profession from your trade, Class from combat and exploration.
Buildings and teachers unlock higher tiers or rare branches across all trees.
Regions influence growth, imprinting elemental or thematic bonuses (Swamp, Volcano, Island, etc.).
Monster kills and expeditions feed the Class tree with essences, abilities, and combat perks.
Profession mastery unlocks recipes, efficiencies, tools, and combat‑adjacent perks tied to your trade.
Task consistency (streaks, categories, difficulty) unlocks lifestyle, focus, momentum, and self‑improvement perks.
Cross‑tree empowerment lets one tree enhance another, creating hybrid builds and personalized growth paths.

Profession Map- each upgrade increases task rewards of that profession by 2% per tier
Novice - Entry-level familiarity; basic tools and methods. XP Required: 0
Trained - Understands fundamentals and completes standard tasks reliably. XP Required: 100
Sought - Recognized for above-average work; people begin requesting your output. XP Required: 300
Adept - Handles difficult or time-consuming tasks with confidence. XP Required: 700
Proficient - Consistent, efficient, and adaptable; understands deeper mechanics. XP Required: 1500
Expert - High-level mastery; innovates and produces exceptional work. XP Required: 3000
Instructor - Able to teach, mentor, and formalize knowledge. XP Required: 6000
Virtuoso - Signature mastery; world-class, recognizable excellence. XP Required: 12000
