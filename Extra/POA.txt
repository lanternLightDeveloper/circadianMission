One‑sentence purpose:
Daily tasks turned into quests and adventures.

Core theme:
This is a daily planner/event manager. It will give or create quest based on things you have to do that day.
At the end of the day it will generate a story you can read or participate in like a choose your own quest. A cozy way to end or start the day.

Project Ideas:
FFT style party expeditions
Daily tasks and big quests (throw in real life facts about the real world)
Mini games
group of regular tasks(maybe collect them from a place like a tavern or quest giver)
professions (with real life job facts? like learning about the transit system) for your party members
join/invite friends in tasks
Leveling + Class system (could be related to real job or game job)
